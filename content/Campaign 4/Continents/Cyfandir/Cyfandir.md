 ​This is the major continent where the campaign takes place. Hundreds of years ago it entered an Era of Divination, and the pantheon began to monopolize the continent with their own isolated nation-states. When the rest of the world refused to follow suit, they isolated the inhabitants to cement their presence and dissuade any desertion.
