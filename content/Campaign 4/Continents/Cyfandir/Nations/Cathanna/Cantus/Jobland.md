### Joblin's List:
- *"Mrs. Wickler says she's got a funky looking gorilla that's taken over her barn and is emasculating her husband."*
	- **Reward** - 80 gold
- *"[[Miss Eldastaff]] has got some weeds in her garden and her back hurts. That's it."*
	- **Reward** - 5 gold and some lemonade
- *"Mr. Woman says there's this gaggle—his words—gaggle of aberrations or perhaps ugly fey that've been hanging around the edge of the forest and beckoning his kid. He also mentioned that at night they coalesce into a mound of flesh that just sits there at the edge of the forest in perfect view from their window."*
	- **Reward** - 25 gold and a free dinner of your choice
