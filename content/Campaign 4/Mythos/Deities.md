
#### The Gods and Goddesses featured in this campaign will primarily be the 36 from the Forgotten Realms:

## Deities of The Forgotten Realms
- ### Auril
	- Neutral evil goddess of winter. Auril's symbol is a six-pointed snowflake
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Nature and Survival
	- Stage 2: Resistance to cold damage
- ### Azuth
	- Lawful neutral god of wizards. Azuth's symbol is a left hand pointing upward, outlined in fire.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Arcana
	- Stage 2: +1 to Intelligence, Proficiency in Intelligence Saving Throws
- ### Bane
	- Lawful evil god of tyranny. Bane's symbol is a black right hand, thumb and fingers together
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Intimidation
	- Stage 2: +1 to Charisma
- ### Beshaba
	- Chaotic evil goddess of misfortune. Beshaba's symbol is a pair of black antlers
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Slight of Hand
	- Stage 2: Reroll one opponent's dice per long rest
- ### Bhaal
	- Neutral evil god of murder. Bhaal's symbol is a skull surrounded by a ring of blood droplets.
	- Stage 1:  Proficiency in Deception
	- Stage 2: +1 to Strength
- ### Chauntea
	- Neutral good goddess of agriculture. Chauntea's symbol is a sheaf of grain, or a blooming rose over grain.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Nature
	- Stage 2: +1 to Charisma, Proficiency with Herbalism Kit
- ### Cyric
	- Chaotic evil god of lies. Cyric's symbol is a jawless white skull on a purple or black sunburst.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Deception
	- Stage 2: +1 to Charisma, Advantage on Deception Checks
- ### Deneir
	- Neutral good god of writing. Deneir's symbol is a lit candle above an open eye.
	- Stage 1: +1 Language
	- Stage 2:  +1 to Intelligence, Proficiency in Performance
- ### Eldath
	- Neutral good goddess of peace. Eldath's symbol is a waterfall plunging into a still pool.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Persuasion
	- Stage 2: +1 to Charisma, Proficiency in Medicine
- ### Gond
	- True neutral god of craft. Gond's symbol is a cog with four spokes.
	- Stage 1: +2 Tool Proficiencies
	- Stage 2: +1 to Intelligence, +1 Tool Proficiency
- ### Helm
	- Lawful neutral god of protection. Helm's symbol is a staring eye on an upright left gauntlet.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Constitution Saving Throws
	- Stage 2: +1 to Constitution
- ### Ilmater
	- Lawful good god of endurance. Ilmater's symbol is two hands bound at the wrist by a red cord.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Athletics
	- Stage 2: +1 to Constitution or One Free Exhaustion
- ### Kelemvor
	- Lawful neutral god of the dead. Kelemvor's symbol is an upright skeletal arm holding balanced scales.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Religion and History
	- Stage 2: Advantage against Undead
- ### Lathander
	- Neutral good god of birth and renewal. Lathander's symbol is a road travelling into a sunrise.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Religion and History
	- Stage 2: +1 to Wisdom
- ### Leira
	- Chaotic neutral goddess of illusion. Liera's symbol is a triangle, pointing down, containing a swirl of mist.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Deception
	- Stage 2: +1 to Spellcasting Ability
- ### Lliira
	- Chaotic good goddess of joy. Lliira's symbol is a triangle of three six-pointed stars.
	- Stage 1: +1 to Charisma
	- Stage 2: Immune to Frightened Condition
- ### Loviatar
	- Lawful evil goddess of pain. Loviatar's symbol is a nine-tailed barbed scourge.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Intimidation
	- Stage 2: +1 to Strength
- ### Malar
	- Chaotic evil god of the hunt. Malar's symbol is a clawed paw.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Nature
	- Stage 2: Proficiency in Stealth, Advantage with Ranged Weapons
- ### Mask
	- Chaotic neutral god of thieves. Mask's symbol is a black mask.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Sleight of Hand
	- Stage 2: +1 to Dexterity
- ### Mielikki (My-LEE-key)
	- Neutral good goddess of forests. Mielikki's symbol is a unicorn's head.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Nature and Animal Handling
	- Stage 2: +1 to Wisdom
- ### Myrkul
	- Neutral evil god of death. Myrkul's symbol is a white human skull.
	-  Stage 1: Proficiency in Religion and Intimidation
	- Stage 2: Immune to Frightened Condition
- ### Mystra
	- Neutral good goddess of magic. Mystra's symbol is a circle of seven stars, or nine stars encircling a flowing red mist, or a single star.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Arcana
	- Stage 2: +1 to Spellcasting Ability
- ### Oghma
	- True neutral god of knowledge. Oghma's symbol is a blank scroll.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Investigation
	- Stage 2: +1 to Intelligence, Proficiency in History or +1 Language
- ### Savras
	- Lawful neutral god of divination and fate. Savras' symbol is a crystal ball containing many kinds of eyes.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Insight
	- Stage 2: +1 to Wisdom, Proficiency in Perception
- ### Selûne
	- Chaotic good goddess of the moon. Selûne's symbol is a pair of eyes surrounded by seven stars.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Nature
	- Stage 2: +1 to Wisdom or Darkvision 60ft
- ### Shar
	- Neutral evil goddess of darkness and loss. Shar's symbol is a black disk encircled with a border.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Stealth
	- Stage 2: Immune to Blinded Condition
- ### Silvanus
	- True neutral god of wild nature. Silvanus' symbol is an oak leaf.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Nature and Survival
	- Stage 2: Proficiency in Posioner's Kit and Animal Handling
- ### Sune
	- Chaotic good goddess of love and beauty. Sune's symbol is the face of a beautiful red-haired woman.
	- Stage 1: +1 Charisma
	- Stage 2: Proficiency in Persuasion, Advantage on Charming
- ### Talona
	- Chaotic evil goddess of disease and poison. Talona's symbol is three tear drops on a triangle.
	- Stage 1: Resistant to Poisoned Condition, Proficiency in Posioner's Kit
	- Stage 2: Immune to Poisoned Condition
- ### Talos
	- Chaotic evil god of storms. Talos' symbol is three lightning bolts radiating from a central point.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Survival
	- Stage 2: +1 Strength
- ### Tempus
	- True neutral god of war. Tempus' symbol is an upright flaming sword.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in all weapons
	- Stage 2: +1 to Strength
- ### Torm
	- Lawful good god of courage and self-sacrifice. Torm's symbol is a white right gauntlet.
	- Stage 1: Advantage and Proficiency with Improvised Weapons
	- Stage 2: +1 Death Saving Throw, Proficiency in Constitution Saving Throws
- ### Tymora
	- Chaotic good goddess of good fortune and adventure. Tymora's symbol is a face-up coin.
	- Stage 1: Roll one dice of your choice with advantage per long rest
	- Stage 2: Roll two dice of your choice with advantage per long rest or +1 Ability Save DC
- ### Tyr
	- Lawful good god, representing justice and heroism. Tyr's symbol is a pair of balanced scales resting on a warhammer.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Insight
	- Stage 2: +1 Strength, Proficiency in Intimidation
- ### Umberlee
	- Chaotic evil goddess of the sea. Umberlee's symbol is an ocean wave curling left and right.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Intimidation
	- Stage 2: +10 Swimming speed
- ### Waukeen
	- True neutral goddess of trade. Waukeen's symbol is an upright coin with Waukeen's profile facing left.
	- Stage 1: Proficiency in Persuasion
	- Stage 2: +1 Charisma, Guaranteed 10% off price once per long rest
