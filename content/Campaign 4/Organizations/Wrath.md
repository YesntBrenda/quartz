
## Hierarchy:
- #### Barbatos, God of Other Fates
- #### Progenitor Solomon
- #### The Archons
	- Based on the Fates
	- **[[Clotho]]**
		- Youngest of the Archons.
		- Enamored with the visions of other-fated worlds, they specialized in the pulling of these beings and objects to his own. They use it to summon powerful warriors and weapons to act as their protection.
		- Their eyes are almost always bloodshot, their body thin and frail, and their breathing labored and exhausted. 
	- **[[Lachesis]]**
		- Middle of the Archons.
		- They assumed a more passive approach, honing their perception of the other fates to allow for unparalleled movement in battle. They use their gift to easily avoid strikes, discover blind spots, and recognize patterns in their opponents fighting style. 
		- Uses a simple staff for combat
	- **[[Atropos]]**
		- Eldest of the Archons.
		- The wisest of the three recognized the ability to use both the sight of the unfated and its manifestation to create a sort of augmentation magic. They use it to biologically cripple their opponents all while expending little energy.
