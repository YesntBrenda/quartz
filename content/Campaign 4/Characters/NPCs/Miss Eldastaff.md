
Old human woman in Cantus with a beautiful garden that she's been tending to for decades, and unnaturally good lemonade.

Bits of Lore:
- Death keeps trying to take her
- She was once married to an elderly elf man, who passed 13 years prior to the present
- Her lemonade can give you inspiration
- She does not wish to share her secret lemonade recipe until after her death
- The players believe that her deceased husband haunts the drawer that stores the secret formula, protecting it beyond the grave
- I might have the secret recipe to the lemonade be the persistent avatar of a now-dead god

Bits for Roleplay:
- She opens the door VERY slowly
- Stereotypical warm elderly woman voice
