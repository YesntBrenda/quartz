Legendary woodworker that played a major role in the industrialization of the continent "Since Divination", but mysteriously vanished at the height of his acclaim.

## Arc
I want his internal lore to be that he was so well liked and dedicated to the craft, that his acclaim began to rival that of Gond himself. Because of this, he was forced into hiding—else he'd be executed.
The party will find him (Deep in either Selûne's country or Mielikki's) very aged
