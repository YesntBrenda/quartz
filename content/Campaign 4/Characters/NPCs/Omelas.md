Omelas was a Knight of Torm born in the year 60 SD to (Father) and (Mother). He was brutally slaughtered in the year 84 SD for the [[Nation of Eldath]]'s nefarious purposes. Now his soul clings to this plane in the form of a dried corpse rife with malice and vengeance.

On the party's path to Cathanna, Jazmine came into contact with his blood and became a sort of host for Omelas' remnants.
