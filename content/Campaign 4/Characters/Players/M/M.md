### Description
​	"M" is a changeling with trauma-based Disassociative Identity Disorder that causes them to shift into entirely different character sheets once certain conditions are met, or at times completely at random. Any indication that they are shifting alters is intercepted by their brain to ensure that they do not learn this (e.g. selective blindness, selective deafness, et cetera).

### Backstory:
​	[[Archibald Fredrickson]] was a young writer—decent at best—and he was looking to write a masterpiece. He was in extreme debt from local presses, and wasn't making the money he needed to make. He needed to make something momentous, something that would inspire, something to entice! He to be inspired himself.
​	One day, while walking along a beach of [[Nation of Deneir]], he found what appeared to be the blur of a man washed up on shore. No notable features—almost a blank being. Feeling as if it were a sign from the gods, he took the being in.
​	He had a brilliant idea: he’d write a fictional poem based of this person and their life. [[Archibald Fredrickson|Archibald]] nurtured the person until they'd finally awoken—when suddenly, the person became extremely familiar: a near copy of [[Archibald Fredrickson|Archibald]]. Stunned, he began to revaluate whether he should have brought the being in or not. Regardless, he needed to write a story and was desperate for money.
​	[[Archibald Fredrickson|Archibald]] attempted questioning the man, but quickly came to find that the man had no idea who he was, where he was, or why he was here. [[Archibald Fredrickson|Archibald]] took it upon himself, and decided that he’d just make stuff up. And so, he named the man [[Mirdis]] *(based on the great legend of [[Miris]] from a land far away)*.
​	He recounted to the man stories of him falling in love with a woman—[[Mareissa]]—and told of her vengeful ex-boyfriend—[[Mirrisso]]—a savvy Spaniard who never put his love for her above his love for nature. This enraged [[Mareissa]], and she resented [[Mirrisso]] for it. While [[Mirdis]] wasn't a fighter, he had a great way with words. So, when [[Mirrisso]] and [[Mirdis]] had an altercation, [[Mirdis]] insulted and outwitted him until [[Mirrisso]] snapped and struck [[Mirdis]]. [[Mirdis]] was brought within an inch of his life, when a royal paladin—[[Mirwis]]—appeared and saved him from certain doom. *(Due to [[Archibald Fredrickson|Archibald]]'s lack of creativity, all the names wound up resembling that of [[Miris]]).*
​	With this general basis of a story down, he sent the newly named [[Mirdis]] on his way, along with a flyer from [[Theos]] and a draft of his new short story. [[Mirdis]] and his map almost made it to [[Theos]], when something odd happened: he suddenly and randomly went unconscious. The next time he awoke, he was being thrown in prison. [[Mirwis]]—the aspiring young dragonborn—had come taken his place and noticed the flyer. Having no recollection, he said “divine knowledge sounds like it might be interesting...” and continued on the journey.

> [!Spoiler]- 
> What no one else seems to know, is that they actually originated from the world beyond [[Cyfandir]]'s coasts, and arrived within its realm by a solitary ship unfit for the tumultuous seas. As luck would have it, they unknowingly happened to arrive at the same time as a ship from [[Solomon's Island]] was slipping through, allowing the changeling to bypass the typically lethal sea borders of Umberlee. They, however, were not protected from the [[The Great Forgetting]] that blankets the continent—and so lost access to seemingly all of their memories of home.

### Triggers
​	Different environments or scenarios can trigger "M" to shift into different alters. These correspond to secret traumas they've experienced, and the tailored alters are meant to help combat those perceived threats. However, there are also times where "M" shifts and there's no perceptible reason behind it.
##### [[Mirwis]] *(Paladin)*
- **Instantaneous:**
	- Seeing a personal crime against an innocent
- **Delayed:**
	- In an enclosed space lacking natural light
	- In the presence of the undead or demonic
##### [[Mirdis]] *(Bard)*
- **Instantaneous:**
	- Feeling enamored
	- When embarrassed
- **Delayed:**
	- After every long rest
##### [[Mirrisso]] *(Druid)*
- **Instantaneous:**
	- Overabundance of some element (enflamed or flooded area)
- **Delayed:**
	- Surrounded by nature/foliage/animals and is also relaxed
##### [[Mareissa]] *(Barbarian)*
- **Instantaneous:**
	- Witnessing a critical hit
	- In the presence of any dinosaur
	- Outnumbered by at least 2x
- **Delayed:**
	- N/A
##### [[Mirchael]] *(Banker)*
- **Instantaneous:**
	- In the presence of banking topics
- **Delayed:**
	- N/A
