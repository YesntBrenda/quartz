### Backstory:
​	*Fisher Wizard comes from a simple Grung colony in a far off jungle.  Being born red, Fisher was born smarter than most Grungs. He was raised to feel superior than others and learn magic. However, Fisher never really cared about any of that. Grungs wanted strict social order, and to order people they felt were lower than them—but Fisher just wanted to laze around and fish!
​	Using his superior intellect, Fisher secretly built a boat and sailed down the river, and away from his home. He hopped from town to town, fishing and picking up on magic, taking odd jobs whenever they came up. He renamed himself to better fit into the human world, after the two things he knew he was. A Fisher Wizard.*
