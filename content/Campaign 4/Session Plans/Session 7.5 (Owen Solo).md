### Fishing Mechanics
- **Hooking Mechanics:**
	- bait influences fish size/type
	- location influences fish size/type
- **Reeling Mechanics:**
	- Caster: d20 + STR/DEX Modifier + Rod Modifier
		- A Critical Success allows the caster to add an additional roll
		- A Critical Failure adds a tally to the fish's Escape Counter (EC)
	- Fish: d20 + STR Modifier
		- A Critical Success allows the fish to add an additional roll
		- A Critical Failure gives the caster advantage on their next roll
	- Escape Counter
		- When a fish's EC completely fills, they escape the hook and may swim away
- Other Notes:
	- Other players can assist—usually through magical means. This can be through spells like Shape Water, Mage Hand, Minor Illusion, etc.
