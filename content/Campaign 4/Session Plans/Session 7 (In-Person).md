## Rough Outline:

**Okay so the main critiques to keep note of while I'm planning this session are:**
- Allow for more player agency
	- Don't always fully paint a picture for how you imagine the party will interact with something; get the basics (if anything) and then create it as the party interacts with it
- No "Do this or die"
	- e.g. Great Rat Spirit, Deborhai, etc.
- Keep the threats around the party as attainable threats that the party can theoretically overcome
	- e.g. bank rats, Deborhai, etc.
- Make the plot more intertwined with the characters' goals quicker
	- Make sure that the characters have stakes in the overarching plot, that way they aren't just being pulled along.
- More player based objectives when they're reluctant
	- ​Little things work as well, like giving Owen some local fishing holes or whatnot​

- More Exploration?
	- That's what was voted on most in the poll, so please your players
