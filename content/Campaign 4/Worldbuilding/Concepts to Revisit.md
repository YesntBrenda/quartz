- ## Retired Characters
	- I want to provide the option for players to completely retire their characters to some goal, that way their character can work towards something in the background and they themselves begin playing a new character to continue traveling with the party.
	- There would of course need to be setbacks, and I might even add a system that puts the retiring up to change based on how in-character it would be. Mayhaps I set the DC based on the logic of it, and they'd need to roll a D20 on whether they're convicted enough or not. As for the drawbacks, they'd of course lose their whole inventory. I might even say that their next character would need to be a level lower than their previous as a means to give that sacrifice more weight.
